Data sources


Figure NZ
Accessible aggregator of standardised data relating to New Zealand.
data.govt.nz
Data.govt.nz helps people discover, learn and use open data easily; empowering, enabling informed decision making, and problem-solving for citizens and business alike.
Digital NZ
Aggregotor of digital content relating to New Zealand, with a focus on, but not limited to, the cultural sector.
Google Dataset Search
Google search aggregator of datasets from around the world.
OECD Data
Find, compare and share the latest OECD data: charts, maps, tables and related publications.
Australian Open Data

Tools


KNIGHTLAB Toolsuite

Innovative suite of open-source, adaptable, and lightweight tools for media makers and storytellers. Create interactive narratives with a focus on data, time or location from scratch - elegantly and easily.

Go to site

Twine

Twine is an open-source tool for telling interactive, nonlinear stories. You don't need to write any code to create a simple story with Twine, but you can extend your stories with variables, conditional logic, images, CSS, and JavaScript when you're ready.

Go to site

ToPāN

ToPān is Topic-Modelling for everyone: from people without programming knowledge to people that want to build teaching and text-reuse tools and apps based on Topic-Modelling data without having to develop their own tool or having to majorly restructure their textual data.

Go to site
QueryPic
QueryPic provides a new way of seeing, searching and understanding the digitised newspapers made available by Trove and Papers Past. Follow changes over time. Map trends and patterns. Follow a hunch. Frame a question.
Glam Workbench
Here you’ll find a collection of tools and examples to help you work with data from galleries, libraries, archives, and museums (the GLAM sector), focusing on Australia and New Zealand.
Webscraper.io
Web Scraper can extract data from sites with multiple levels of navigation. It can navigate a website on all levels.
Piktochart
Simple, intuitive tool that helps you and your team tell stories with the visual impact they deserve. (Paid service, free tier available
